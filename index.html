Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientists at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic institutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technologies on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests, measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiments includes a control condition ('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
